Many Decentralized Science projects hoist ERC20 tokens on mainnet to tokenize their offerings. Here we pool together their Uniswap V3 Liquidity WETH pairs into Mimisbrunnr, an ERC20 that mints in exchange for LP. Users can than unwrap these tokens in exchange for these tokens. Fees earned by this protocol fund Desci Obsidian operations, and in the near future can be aquired by staking Mimis/WETH in its own Uniswap v3 pool. This provides several desirable properties

  • Functions as a pseudo DeSci ETF - get exposure to the entire deSci value stack with just one token
  • More Attractive to LONG than individual tokens- portfolio effect gives a stronger incentive to LONG than individual tokens
  • better capital efficiency - It will be easier to concentrate MIMIS/WETH as the pairs balance each other over WETH than individual pairs
  • composability - wraps UniswapV3 LP into an ERC20 which can integrate well with many other defi products like collateralized lending etc
